Teaching Statement


My mission is to promote cultural appreciation through dance, while providing a positive and open-minded space for dancers to expand their knowledge in Mexican Folkl√≥rico history and technique. Dancers will develop in depth understanding of different regions in Mexico by distinguishing their music, wardrobe and aesthetic of the regions they are dancing. They will learn about rehearsing etiquette and ways to prolong their career as a dancer. Every dancer that takes my course will learn the importance of properly stretching and warming up to prevent injuries. Dancers will also build community by working with other dancers to workshop routines in and outside of class.
